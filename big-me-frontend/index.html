Welcome to BigMe!

A place to handle your daily feelings!




ASD (Autism Spectrum Disorder): Simply Put - A social and Emotional disorder


Kids with ASD need lot of help post diagnosis to help learn/understand social behaviors, emotions, and how to process them. What about parents, we forget sometimes about the ones that may sometimes have no outlet.


Thats where BigMe comes into play. This is a place for parents to voice their feelings, a place to help them process what daily life is like raising a child with ASD. Don't be Shy, Share and remember... You are not alone.
